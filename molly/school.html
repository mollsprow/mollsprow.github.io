UXDI - Introduction to User Experience

User Experience I is a week intensive course designed to introduce you to the world of user experience. As a group we developed an idea for a product to enhance and promote the experience of students and prospective students of the MBS program, while learning how to develop that idea into a successful prototype. Touching on the basics of contextual inquiry, personas and journey mapping, prototyping and user testing, we ended the week by presenting our idea along with the roadmap and findings of our work during the week.

UXDIII - Usability Evaluation

Usability Evaluation delves deep into the theories of heuristics, data presentation, best practices of user testing, and working with live and remote participants. As a group, we evaluated the desktop version of Google Maps using ISO 9241 Dialogue Principles. Following this, we conducted two sets of usability studies using our evaluation as guide to further examine the faults and successes of the application.
